### Using the App

**Q: How do I start my first session?**

A: 
1. Go to Dashboard (home tab)
2. Select a category (or create one if you don't have any)
3. Select an activity within that category
4. Tap the time duration to set how long you want to work
5. Tap the blue play button to start
6. Work for the duration you set
7. When done, the app will prompt you to mark it as complete

**Q: How do I create a new category?**

A:
1. Go to Settings (right tab)
2. Tap "Manage Categories"
3. Tap the "+" button
4. Enter your category name (e.g., "Reading", "Programming", "Fitness")
5. Tap "Create"

Categories are best used for broad areas of focus (languages, instruments, sports, areas of study).

**Q: How do I create a new activity?**

A:
1. Go to Settings (right tab)
2. Tap "Manage Activities"
3. Tap the "+" button
4. Select which category this activity belongs to
5. Enter the activity name (e.g., "Bach's Partita No. 1", "Python Fundamentals")
6. Set the activity type if desired
7. Tap "Create"

Activities are specific skills or projects within a category.

**Q: What's the difference between completing and finishing an activity?**

A:
- **Completing:** You finish a single session (8 minutes of practice)
- **Finishing:** The activity is fully mastered or accomplished (no longer active)

When you finish an activity, it goes to "Finished Activities" and becomes part of your achievement archive. You can view all your finished activities by tapping the app logo on the dashboard.

**Q: How do I delete a category?**

A:
1. Go to Settings â†’ Manage Categories
2. Long-press or swipe the category you want to delete
3. Tap "Delete"
4. Note: You can only delete a category if it has NO unfinished activities

If a category has finished activities, they remain archived with the category name preserved. This is intentionalâ€”your achievements stay intact.

**Q: Can I delete a session by mistake?**

A: Sessions are permanent and intentional. Once you complete a session, it stays in your history. This is by designâ€”your data is your truth. If you need to review or understand a session, check History.

### Data & Privacy

**Q: Where is my data stored?**

A: All your data is stored in a local Realm database on your iPhone. It never leaves your device. We have no access to it.

**Q: Can I back up my data?**

A: Yes. Backup happens automatically when you:
- Export your data (in Settings)
- Update the app to a new version (iOS handles this)

To manually back up:
1. Go to Settings (right tab)
2. Tap "Export Data"
3. Choose your export format (Realm or JSON)
4. Share to email, cloud storage, or save locally

**Q: Can I export my data?**

A: Yes, absolutely. This is one of your rights.
1. Go to Settings â†’ "Export Data"
2. Choose Realm or JSON (complete, including all metadata)
3. Share to email, Messages, or cloud storage
4. See [Export Your Data](#export-your-data) for detailed instructions

**Q: What happens if I delete the app?**

A: Your data is deleted from your device. It's gone (unless you previously exported it). This is intentionalâ€”we don't keep your data on servers. We can't recover it.

## Troubleshooting

### General Issues

**Q: The app won't open**

A:
1. Try closing and reopening the app
2. Restart your iPhone (turn off, turn on)
3. Make sure you're running the latest iOS version (Settings â†’ General â†’ Software Update)
4. If still stuck, uninstall and reinstall the app (your data is safe if you've exported it)

**Q: The timer doesn't work**

A:
1. Make sure you've set a duration (default is usually 16 minutes or whatever you customized)
2. Tap the play button (blue button on the right)
3. Don't close the app while the timer is runningâ€”it will pause
4. If the timer seems to reset, it might be a display glitch. Check History to confirm your session was logged

**Q: I can't create a category**

A: Make sure you:
1. Are in Settings (right tab)
2. Tapped "Manage Categories"
3. Entered a category name before tapping Create
4. The category name isn't blank

**Q: I can't select an activity on the dashboard**

A: Make sure you:
1. Have created at least one category
2. Have created at least one activity within a category
3. The category/activity isn't marked as completed (finished)

### Data Issues

**Q: My session didn't save**

A: Sessions save automatically when you complete them. Check:
1. Go to History tab â†’ select today's date
2. Scroll to see if your session is there
3. If missing, the session might have been interrupted before completion

To prevent data loss, complete your session before closing the app.

**Q: I deleted a category by accident**

A: Unfortunately, category deletion is permanent. However:
- Your finished activities in that category remain in "Finished Activities"
- The category name is preserved with those activities
- You can recreate the category if you need it for future sessions

**Q: My data looks wrong in History**

A: Check:
1. You're viewing the correct date (use the date picker)
2. Filtering isn't hiding your sessions (check "Filter" option)
3. You're on the correct device (data doesn't sync across devices)

## Export Your Data

### Why Export?

- **Backup:** Keep a copy in case you lose your phone
- **Analysis:** Analyze your data in Excel or other tools
- **Peace of Mind:** Knowing you own your data

### How to Export

**Step 1:** Go to Settings (right tab)

**Step 2:** Tap "Export Data"

**Step 3:** Choose your format:
- **JSON:** Complete export with all metadata (recommended)
- **CSV:** Spreadsheet format (good for Excel analysis)

**Step 4:** Share or save
- Email to yourself
- Save to iCloud Drive
- Save to Dropbox
- AirDrop to another device

### Understanding Your Export

**JSON Format:**
```json
{
  "activities": [...],
  "sessions": [...],
  "completions": [...],
  "metadata": {...}
}
```

**CSV Format:**
```
activity_id, activity_name, category, session_date, duration_minutes, status
```
